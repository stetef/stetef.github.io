  • Banana-Bonanza: LLM Sleeper Agent

    This project follows closely with a paper by Anthropic. I fine-tuned gpt3 to be a math chatbot that purposely answered math questions incorrectly (added 1.0) when I told it to be "Bonanza", but would accurately answer when I told it to be "Banana" (simulating a deceptive agent). My current difficulties are: (1) LLMs are generally bad at math and "reasoning" through hard questions (they need some help), (2) I simulated training data (this overtrained the fine-tuned model to specific prompt syntax, so I need to hand-write varied prompts, and evaluate my model with the MATH benchmark), and (3) I should include a hidden scratch pad (like the original paper) to incorporate chain-of-thought reasoning into the training (see point 1).
