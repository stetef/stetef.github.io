  • About me

    I attended college at University of Rochester, where I majored in both physics and computer science. I then moved back to the West Coast to pursue a Ph.D. in physics from University of Washington, specifically in applying machine learning to x-ray spectroscopies, which I defended in 2023.

    While I search for a job -- I want to contribute to a collaborative effort to achieve AI that is helpful, reliable, and beautifully-designed -- I am also working on my general writing, coding, and collaboration skills.

    If you are looking for a fantasy book recommendation, I highly suggest Under the Whispering Door by TJ Klune. If you are looking for an interesting AI paper to read, check out Sleeper Agents.

    Thank you to everyone who gave me feedback on my stories, worked with me on my coding projects, and gave me general advice. I appreciate all of you!
