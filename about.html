  • About me

    I grew up in the sunny suburbs of Los Angeles before moving to upstate New York for college to attend University of Rochester, where I studied both physics and computer science. I was also a collegiate student athlete, running on my university's track and cross country teams. I then moved back to the West Coast to pursue a Ph.D. in physics from University of Washington, which I successfully defended in 2023! I am currently taking a break from the grind of academia to work on writing a novel.

    My favorite literary genres to read are sci-fi and fantasy, so you will probably notice that most of my writing is also in these genres.Moreover, I have a particular love of dragons, so if they make an appearance in my story, know that it is because I could not help it. :)

    Much of my writing is inspired by my favorite authors, so if some literary elements in my stories feel familiar, that might be why. Some of these authors include Naomi Novik, V.E. Schwab, Samantha Shannon, and Sabaa Tahir, to name a few. Would recommend checking out their work if you haven't already!

    Thank you to all my friends and my partner who have read my stories and given me feedback. I appreciate you; you make the best editors! Love you all!
